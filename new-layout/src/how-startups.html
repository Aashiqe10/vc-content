How Startups can take advantages by applying e-commerce platforms

For any startup there is a choice to use existent e-commerce solution or design and develop the own one. It is often seeming that in-house solution fits better for MVP, however using existent solutions may bring your vision to MVP much faster.

How Startups can take advantages by applying e-commerce platforms

1
Out-of-the box solutions don’t fit startups
2
Licensing doesn’t work for startup’s business models
3
It is hard to find qualified experts to achieve MVP fast

Startups assumes a breaking business model based on either new technology or new business approaches. Since the startup doing things different using e-commerce, it faces a challenge: inflexible out-of-the-box solutions doesn’t fit the innovative model by design, it is very expensive to change it, it is too expensive for MVP.

Traditional licensing would be another challenge. It is purposed for traditional business models and practices, but not for companies with disruptive business models.
For example, building your own industry or market specific platform for further re-selling in some format (SaaS, marketplace etc.) is usually locked not only by technical weakness of “native” e-commerce solution but also by their licensing policy, which makes impossible for startups to earn money.

The third critical problem would be the lack of relevant experience and expertise in MVP delivery. Startup MVP is about minimizing time and costs. It is required to select the shortest way, which is not always obvious, and move forward as fast as possible.

It is required to find a reliable partner who will handle these risks: help to define MVP scope properly so that business model will be implemented the cheapest way and delivered timely

How Startups can take advantages by applying e-commerce platforms

Starting with inflexible platform takes a lot of efforts on every pivoting
A lot of time and efforts to deliver product which doesn’t bring traction
In-house solution means critical dependencies on technical employees

Selecting the inflexible platform as a basis, startup faces the requirement to invest a lot into development or business model adoption and will need to re-build a solution every time when pivoting.

There is a big risk that a lot of time and efforts will be spend on delivering functionality which is not valuable for customers and doesn’t bring traction. Even if it would be possible to create a desirable functionality at the end, it will take significant additional time and money loss.

The lack of experience on selecting e-commerce solution may kill startup due to listed problems. Facing these challenges startups often go to building a unique in-house solution because it looks deceitfully cheap.

In fact this step often brings same problems (too long, too expensive) together with additional overheads, such as a risk of dependency on internal technical specialists who are the only people in the world who knows how to maintain the created in-house solution.

How Startups can take advantages by applying e-commerce platforms

Due to Customizable & Extensible Platform and Modular architecture the Virto Commerce platform is designed to be adoptive and flexible. We provide enablers for building up a solution of any complexity on top of the platform with minimum time, costs and risks.

Based on our experience with startups we realize the licenses-related challenges which startups face with. Being a market disruptor, we respect and facilitate market disruptors. That is why we developed the specific licensing models which should fit startups and companies who are aiming to apply new business models and practices into e-commerce market.

Virto Commerce company has successful experience in facilitating startups MVP and we share our experience (both technical and management) with our partners and customers.

Our partners together with Virto Commerce company helps to startups to resolve the expertise issue. We help our clients to select the implementation partner if required and engage our experts when required to guarantee a project success.
