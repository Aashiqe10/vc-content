Hot to overcome main e-commerce challenges faced by mid-market companies?

Mid-market companies are already having high and constantly growing business complexity (which might be close to the enterprise level) while still don’t have enterprise-scale budgets. In terms of e-commerce it means that all projects should be very result-focused, provide good ROI and still deliver a reliable basis for the further growth.

Top Ecommerce Challenges Faced by Mid-Market Companies

1
Out-of-the box solutions don’t fit startups
2
Licensing doesn’t work for startup’s business models
3
It is hard to find qualified experts to achieve MVP fast

Startups assumes a breaking business model based on either new technology or new business approaches. Since the startup doing things different using e-commerce, it faces a challenge: inflexible out-of-the-box solutions doesn’t fit the innovative model by design, it is very expensive to change it, it is too expensive for MVP.

Traditional licensing would be another challenge. It is purposed for traditional business models and practices, but not for companies with disruptive business models.
For example, building your own industry or market specific platform for further re-selling in some format (SaaS, marketplace etc.) is usually locked not only by technical weakness of “native” e-commerce solution but also by their licensing policy, which makes impossible for startups to earn money.

The third critical problem would be the lack of relevant experience and expertise in MVP delivery. Startup MVP is about minimizing time and costs. It is required to select the shortest way, which is not always obvious, and move forward as fast as possible.

It is required to find a reliable partner who will handle these risks: help to define MVP scope properly so that business model will be implemented the cheapest way and delivered timely

Top Ecommerce Implementation Risks for Mid-Market Companies

A
Wrong solution choice and problems with implementation
B
When project goes out of budget, companies sacrifice other valuable investments to continue
C
Lost opportunities due to freezing a lot of money for long implementation budgets

It is required to attract professionals to run e-commerce project of that scale in a proper way. The lack of deep e-commerce expertise often leads to the choice of the solution which doesn’t fit the business. The lack of experience in planning and execution of agile implementation projects leads to long, unpredictable projects, money loss and, as a result, very slow investments return.

Implementation budget is limited, and mid-market companies often don’t have free money flow. It means that every time when the project goes out of the budget the management should either freeze the project until money appears or sacrifice some other investments to make a project continue. Both decisions are painful decisions which makes a company growth slower.

When implementation team doesn’t have an experience in agile projects to deliver short valuable implementation projects, the company needs to freeze a lot of money to run a global, long, expensive implementation project. This kind of money freezing is especially painful for mid-market companies with the limited cashflow which should feed the opportunities for growing business.

How Does Virto Commerce Resolves These Risks?

Finding an experienced implementation partner is a good way to resolve the lack of deep e-commerce expertise problem. The risk here is to face with the money-focused partner, who will use their expertise to take as much money as possible from the client. Finding an experiences and reliable partner would be the solution.

Virto Commerce company pays a lot of attention to partners. Having successful experience of making custom implementation ourselves we understand that the quality of our partners is critical for the customer. We may take responsibility for finding a trustable implementation partner or we also can train your trusted provider to work with Virto Commerce and delegate our experts to facilitate your project.

Virto Commerce solution is designed to facilitate the agile approach to e-commerce platform implementation. Customizable and extensible platform and modular architecture makes possible to start implementation with the small projects, focused on adding new functions, processes, regions and then continue by gradual extending the solution.

Small projects allow to start getting returns on investments fast, it doesn’t require to sacrifice other investments a lot to start implementation. Small projects also mean that budget errors would be small even if it happens and it will not a big struggle to compensate it to make a project go life.
