About us

Our Story

cleverbridge was founded in 2005 by industry experts with decades of ecommerce experience. Today, we provide ecommerce technology, services and expertise for hundreds of software and cloud companies selling digital products to consumers and businesses. Leveraging our hosted solution and full-service offering, our clients strategically use ecommerce to build stronger customer relationships and rapidly increase global online sales.

Our Team

cleverbridge is an innovative, enthusiastic, open-minded community of more than 280 technophiles collaborating across offices in Cologne, Chicago, San Francisco and Tokyo. Though we’re a diverse group, personally and professionally, we’re all united in our focus on two very important things:
1) exceeding the expectations of our clients and 2) having fun with what we do, both in the office and outside of it.
